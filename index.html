About the Game

Endless Obstacle is a fast-paced arcade game where you survive increasingly difficult obstacles while collecting points. Navigate through Naruto-themed environments with smooth 60 FPS gameplay and multiple character choices. How high can you score?

🎮 Game Features

⚡ Smooth Gameplay

60 FPS rendering with 150 updates per second for responsive controls

🎭 Multiple Characters

Play as Naruto, Sasuke, or Sakura with unique animations

🎯 Progressive Difficulty

Obstacles spawn faster as your score increases

💾 Score Persistence

Your high score is automatically saved and loaded

🎵 Dynamic Audio

Character-triggered sound effects with collision feedback

📱 Polished UI

Professional menu system with pause and game-over screens

🕹️ How to Play

Game Controls

  • ← → Arrow Keys - Move left/right
  • Space - Jump (avoid obstacles)
  • ESC - Pause game
  • Mouse - Navigate menus and choose character

Survive as long as possible! Each obstacle you avoid increases your score. Reach milestone scores at 500, 1000, and 1500 points for bonus effects!
